### Investment Analysis Report: July 6, 2026

**Selected Company: SK Hynix**

**Rationale:**
SK Hynix is the optimal investment choice due to its unrivaled technical leadership in High-Bandwidth Memory (HBM) and its strategic position as an essential supplier to Nvidia. As the industry standard for AI accelerators, its production capacity is sold out through 2026, positioning the company as the premier "pure-play" beneficiary of the global AI data center build-out. Furthermore, the upcoming July 10, 2026, $29 billion Nasdaq ADR listing serves as a transformative catalyst, providing significant liquidity and access to deep AI-focused capital markets to accelerate their R&D roadmap for HBM4 technology.

***

**Companies Not Selected:**

**Samsung Electronics**
While Samsung Electronics is a formidable semiconductor powerhouse experiencing a historic profit renaissance, it was not selected as the primary investment over SK Hynix for the following reasons:
*   **Lack of Concentration:** Samsung’s business model is significantly more diversified across foundry, logic chips, and consumer electronics. While this provides a hedge, it dilutes the direct exposure to the high-growth HBM sector that characterizes the current AI infrastructure revolution.
*   **Strategic Focus:** SK Hynix’s singular focus on maintaining market dominance in the HBM stack provides a more targeted investment profile. Samsung’s broader operational structure introduces risks related to intense foundry competition and cyclical volatility inherent in its non-memory segments, which makes it a secondary choice compared to the concentrated, high-alpha potential of SK Hynix’s current market position.
